I am a Masters of Library and Information Science student at Pratt Institute. As a library professional, I aim to contribute to the accessibility and discoverability of information, especially as it relates to underrepresented groups in the archive. The projects on this site, which were completed as a part of my graduate degree studies in Library and Information Science at Pratt Institute, demonstrate my commitment considering intersectional feminist guidelines within information studies. As a librarian and information professional, I hope to produce work that centers care, emotional labor, and invisible labor within the wider framework of decolonization.
